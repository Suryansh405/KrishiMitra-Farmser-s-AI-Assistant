About Krishi Mitra

Empowering Farmers with AI Technology

Our Mission

Krishi Mitra is dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture through artificial intelligence and modern technology. We aim to empower farmers with data-driven insights, helping them make informed decisions about crop management, disease detection, and resource optimization.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI-powered agricultural assistant platform, making advanced farming technology accessible to every farmer, regardless of their location or resources. We envision a future where sustainable farming practices are the norm, powered by intelligent technology.

What We Offer

AI Chat Assistant

Multi-modal AI that understands text and images for comprehensive farming advice

Disease Detection

Advanced computer vision to identify crop diseases and provide treatment solutions

Weather Advisory

Real-time weather-based farming recommendations for optimal crop management

Resource Calculator

Calculate water, fertilizer, and resource requirements with precision

Sustainability Tips

Eco-friendly farming practices for long-term environmental health

Knowledge Base

RAG-powered system with government schemes and agricultural data
